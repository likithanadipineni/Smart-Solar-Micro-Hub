# 🌞 Smart Solar Micro Hub

## 📌 Overview
Smart Solar Micro Hub is a web-based application that helps users analyze solar energy potential and its impact on cost savings and the environment. It acts as a smart decision-support tool for adopting solar energy in homes, colleges, and small communities.

The project focuses on promoting renewable energy by providing simple, data-driven insights through an interactive dashboard.

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Many households, institutions, and small businesses face:
- Frequent power cuts  
- Lack of awareness about solar energy benefits  
- No clear estimation of cost savings and ROI  
- Limited access to simple solar analysis tools  

---

## 💡 Solution
Smart Solar Micro Hub provides an intelligent platform that:
- Estimates solar energy generation  
- Calculates annual electricity savings  
- Shows CO₂ emission reduction  
- Suggests optimal solar system capacity

## ⚙️ How It Works
1. User enters:
   - Roof area  
   - Monthly electricity bill  

2. The system calculates:
   - Solar capacity (kW)  
   - Annual energy generation (kWh)  
   - Estimated savings (₹)  
   - CO₂ reduction  

3. Results are displayed through an interactive dashboard.

---

## 🧮 Basic Calculation Logic
Solar Capacity (kW) = Roof Area × 0.1
Annual Energy = Capacity × 4.5 × 365
Savings = Energy × Electricity Rate
CO₂ Saved = Energy × 0.82
